# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## Do's
- Maintain a professional profile and bio
- Share useful and positive content
- Respect others' opinions
- Protect personal information
- Think before posting

## Don'ts
- Do not post offensive or abusive content
- Do not share fake news or misinformation
- Do not leak personal or academic information
- Do not use inappropriate language
- Do not argue publicly online
