# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Never share OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone
- Do not click suspicious links or unknown messages
- Use strong and unique passwords for accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Verify website URLs before entering personal details
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial or important work

## UPI/Financial Safety Tips
- Never scan QR codes to receive money (QR is only for paying)
- Always verify payment requests before approving transactions
- Use official banking and UPI apps only
- Check transaction details before entering UPI PIN

## Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
- Report fraud immediately to prevent financial loss
